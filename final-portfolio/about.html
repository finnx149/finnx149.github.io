====================================================================================================

		

	Garrett is a Senior at the University of Minnesota. He switched majors from Computer
	Science	to a Bachelor of Individualized Studies at the beginning of his Junior year 
	and says "it's basically 3 minors that make a major". Those minors are Computer Science,
	Interdisciplinary Design, and Asian Language and Literature.
	
	He is proficient in a multitude of programming languages, from C++ to OCaml, and is in
	the process of learning the intricacies of HTML, CSS, and web design. He has completed
	coursework on 3D Graphics, VR Interaction, and Artificial Intelligence. Garrett also has 
	a intermediate-level grasp on Japanese, having taken four semesters of it across his 
	Sophomore and Junior year.
	
	In his free time, Garrett is an avid player of games, both on a computer and on the
	tabletop. He also prototypes games on the side, and has made various private modifications
	of the game Arma 3. He creates pixel art for his games and draws a little on the side.
