====================================================================================================

		

	I can't claim credit for this idea. It was suggested by my online friend "Goober". I 
	found some references and a seamless pattern tutorial, booted up Photoshop, and made 
	a Hawaiian shirt pattern.
			
	The process for creating retextures is fairly straighforward:
		- Get the textures of the uniform/item you want to work on 
		- Cut out sections of the texture to easily recolor or add patterns to them
		- Save and convert to .paa format
		- Write the config and pack it into a format usable by Arma 3
